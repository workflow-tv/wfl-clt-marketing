About

At Workflow, we get that production is a complex process.

We also get that valuable time and revenue are lost due to inefficient, old-style, manual processes. Current systems are resource heavy, paper-based, time consuming and labour intensive. Communication is often fragmented across multiple channels and information is siloed.

With all of this in mind, we have looked at ways to make production simpler and more integrated – all online.

Workflow provides your teams with the tools to work more efficiently, more stream-lined and more cost-effectively.

Workflow will maximise the teams’ time and keep job costs down. Paperless and contact free.

With a mobile app for your clients, cast and crew, Workflow is where Production works.

Features

Faster, more efficient production means more savings in your budget.

Simply put, Workflow’s functions and features rock. Designed to maximise your productivity, these handy functions will help you to streamline your production process from prep to wrap.

Production Booklet
Pulling information from individual sections of your Job, the Production Booklet is published online and is easy to view and read. Distributed to all at the push of a button, it is easily accessible on your laptop or mobile device.

Just like that.

Daily Call Sheets
While we can’t pretend that we have removed the often interminable wait for confirmation of the next day’s call time, we have made it much easier and more streamlined to create and update your daily call sheet – and to share it online with everyone in an instant.
Locations
All your locations in one place, online, including special customised notes for Unit Parking – and with GPS co-ordinates linked to a range of Map Apps for instant directions. No more late-night map drawing for Unit Managers, no more dangerous dash across the highway in the dark to post location signs before sunrise, no more unsafe driving-while-map-reading for your drivers. Click and go.
Supplier Directory
All your suppliers’ details all in one place. No need to search elsewhere for contact details. Or VAT numbers. Or recapture info for call sheets and production booklets. Simply search and add to your documents. Use ours. Add your own. They are all there, at the touch of a button.
Crew and Cast Members Directory
All your crew and cast contact details all in one place. No need to try and remember phone numbers. Or personal details. Or recapture each crew member on every job. Again and again. Simply search and add crew and cast to your production documents.
Notifications
Too many whatsapp groups? Endless phone calls wasting your time? Send instant notifications direct from the system. Send to one person, send to a whole department or send to everyone on the job. See who has received and read the notification. Send again if necessary. All at the click of a button.
Time Sheets
No more manual signing of time sheets on pieces of paper attached to clip boards. Crew and cast log onto the mobile app, enter their own times, and submit. All the production team has to do is approve, download and send to Agents. It’s really that simple.
File Storage
All your job files and production documents, all in one place, reliably, securely, and accessible to all at any time, as and when needed. Before, during and even long after the job.
